# Resume_Analyzer

**NOTE:** Our Resume analyzer project ended in **2001**, with an ancient version of VisualText and NLP++. The project emphasized development of the RUG automated rule generation capability (a prototype capability in VisualText).  Please **don't judge VisualText and NLP++** by this prehistoric analyzer!  Instead, look at **TAIParse** as an example of the things you can do with VisualText and NLP++.

An advanced resume analysis prototype is available via download from our website.  It extracts contact information, experience and education records from resumes at 80% accuracy (90% precision and 75% recall/completeness).  It is available at

[http://dev.textanalysis.com/Rez0_3_1.zip](http://dev.textanalysis.com/Rez0_3_1.zip) (latest compiled version)
